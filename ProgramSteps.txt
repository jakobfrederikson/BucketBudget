BucketBudget
>> Ultimate goal:
> Create/download/upload style website. No user accounts.
> Create a budget.
> Download the budget as a JSON file.
> Upload the JSON file to edit your budget.

>> MVP - Finished.
> Create a budget.
> Income(s) (something/someone that earns money for the budget).
> Expense (something that takes money out of the budget).
> Frequency (is this a weekly, fortnightly, monthly or yearly budget).
> Bucket (Net income, after expenses, divided into buckets).
-> Buckets know to say '$X.XX into [X] Bucket per [Frequency]'.
-> Buckets always add to the net income.

Budgets can have:
- One frequency
- One owner
- One created date
- One title
- Many members
- Many incomes
- Many expenses
- Many buckets

Incomes can have:
- a title
- an amount
- a frequency
- the ability to distuinguish if the buckets should be split for this income

Expenses can have:
- a title
- an amount
- a frequency
- the ability to distuinguish if the expense is an important bill or not 

Users can have:
- One username
- One password
- Many budgets
